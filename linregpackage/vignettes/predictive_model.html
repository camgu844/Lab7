We can see that with lmfit the model provide better fitting, but with high number of parameters, this model will have low generalization ability and overfitting training data.

Conversely, lmforward selected 4 parameters give reasonable good RMSE, which is 5.097917. From this point of view, 9 more parameters included in lmfit may only explained 0.072 RMSE value.

We can calculate Adjusted \(R^2\) to make selection between 2 model, given by formula: \[ AdjR^2 = 1-\frac{SS_{residuals}/(n-K)}{SS_{total}/(n-1)} \] Which clearly show that lmforward is better choice.
